  About Me





Hey there! My name is Koh Yi Joshua. I am currently an undergraduate pursuing a degree in computer science at Singapore's Nanyang Technological University. I am a multi-skilled and versatile individual with various tools under my belt, and am intrinsically motivated to learn more to empower myself.
